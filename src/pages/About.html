ABOUT US

Enriching lives of IIT Delhi Alumni, giving back to IITD and Nation Building Initiatives! IITD Alumni Association is dedicated to bringing together the alumni community on a common platform to build another channel of personal and professional support to members through 'self-help' within the community. IIT Delhi Alumni Association today has a membership of over 54,000 graduates from our alma mater and is growing at about 2,000 members a year.
